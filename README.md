# Gravity
 <br>
A simplist runner for Windows, linux and MacOs <br><br>

**Command** <br>
Left    :   *Left-Key*  <br>
Right   :   *Right-Key*  <br>
jump    :   *space*  <br>

**Score** <br>
When you're on the ground your score goes down <br>
When you're on the sky your score goes up <br>
